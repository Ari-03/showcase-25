2024 HDSI Capstone Showcase

Poster session for the undergraduate data science major's senior capstone program

Friday, March 15th, 2024, 11AM-2:30PM

Price Center East Ballroom, UC San Diego Campus

Note the updated location!


The event is split into two blocks – Block 1, from 11AM-12:30PM, and Block 2, from 1-2:30PM. Each project will only be presented in one block. Tickets have already sold out.

With questions, contact Suraj Rampure at rampure@ucsd.edu.

This year, we have 71 project groups spread across a variety of topics. The showcase is divided into two blocks based on topics, 11AM-12:30PM and 1-2:30PM. The showcase will operate as a poster session, where attendees can approach the projects that interest you and ask their teams questions.

Below, you'll find the posters, websites, reports, and code repositories for all projects.

Jump to: Block 1 (11-12:30PM) • Block 2 (1-2:30PM)
